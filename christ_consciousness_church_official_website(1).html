Statement of Belief

Christ Consciousness Church affirms the sincere religious belief that the Kingdom of Heaven is within all men and women. Each person carries an inner divine spark and is capable of direct communion with the Most High Source of All Creation.

Religious Practices

  • Prayer, meditation, and contemplation
  • Ethical living through love, forgiveness, and compassion
  • Teaching inspired by the life and example of Jesus Christ
  • Remote worship through meditation and spiritual focus

Worship Services

Service Schedule: One worship service per month, held on the 3rd day of every month.

Duration: Approximately three (3) hours.

Services may be attended in person or remotely through prayer, meditation, and spiritual practice.

Service Calendar

The highlighted date marks the monthly worship service (3rd of the month).

Trust & Governance

Christ Consciousness Church is held and administered through a religious trust organized exclusively for religious purposes. The Trust ensures that all property and activities are devoted primarily and dominantly to worship, religious instruction, and sanctuary.

This Church operates on a not-for-profit basis. No part of its assets or income inures to private benefit. Governance decisions are made by the Trustees in accordance with fiduciary duty, conscience, and applicable law.

This page is provided for transparency to the public and to municipal assessors reviewing religious use.

Private Sanctuary

The Church maintains a private religious sanctuary for prayer, meditation, and spiritual retreat. Access is governed by Church doctrine and religious discipline.

Use of the sanctuary supports religious contemplation, instruction, and spiritual communion, consistent with sincerely held religious beliefs.
